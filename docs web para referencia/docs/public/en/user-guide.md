# Quick Start Guide

Everything you need to know to manage your projects and finances in minutes.

## 1. Create your first Project

Organize your ideas into concrete projects.

1.  Go to **'My Projects'** from the side menu.
2.  Click the **'+ Create Project'** button.
3.  Choose a template (Freelancer, Startup) or Custom.

## 2. Register a Transaction

Keep a detailed record of income and expenses for each project.

1.  Enter a project and go to the **'Finance'** tab.
2.  Use the **'Add Income'** or **'Add Expense'** button.
3.  Select the category and save.

## 3. Invite your Team

Work together in real-time.

1.  In your project, go to the **'Members'** tab.
2.  Click **'Invite Member'** and enter their email.
3.  Assign a role (Admin or Member) and send.

---

> **Pro Tip:** You can customize which widgets to see on your dashboard by clicking the "Customize" button in the top right corner.
